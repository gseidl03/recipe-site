  1. Start by pouring the flour out onto a surface and make a well in the middle.

  2. Crack your eggs into the center with a pinch of salt.

  3. Gradually start combining the eggs into the flour using a fork.

  4. Begin kneading the dough for 4-7 minutes or until the dough springs back when poked with your finger.

  5. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.

  6. Unwrap dough and cut into 4 quarters, leaving 3 covered.

  7. Roll dough until it reaches appropriate thickness.

  8. Slice into strips 1/2" wide and dust in flour.
